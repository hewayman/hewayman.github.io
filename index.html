Hannah Wayman

Hi, I'm Hannah. I'm a frontend developer based out of Indianapolis. I am a problem-solver, an art enthusiast, a leader, a barista, a mother, and a wife.

I started out my career tutoring high school students with developmental, mental health, and learning challenges. Over the years, I realized that I could combine my passion for art with my knack for critical thinking.

As I approached my 30s (yikes) with two little darlings at home, I jumped headfirst into learning software and web development. Although there were days that I felt overwhelmed with all the information I was taking in, I learned how capable I am at balancing family time, work, and life-long learning.
